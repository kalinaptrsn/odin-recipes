Balsamic Fig Chicken

Sweet and savory perfection

This dish is a delectable mix of flavors. Fig jam, balsamic vinegar, chicken stock, onion, and fresh rosemary combine to create the perfect sauce to drizzle over the rest of the dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Yukon gold potatoes
  • 10 oz chicken cutlets
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 oz mixed greens
  • 1/4oz rosemary
  • 1 lemon
  • 5 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp fig jam
  • 1 chicken stock concentrate
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Dice potatoes and toss onto a baking sheet with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast, tossing halfway through, about 20-25 minutes.
  4. While potatoes roast, mince shallot. Strip 1 tsp rosemary leaves from stems, and finely chop the leaves.
  5. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper; add to pan and cook 3-5 minutes per side.
  6. Once chicken is cooked through, transfer to a cutting board and lower heat to medium.
  7. Add shallot, chopped rosemary, and a drizzle of olive oil to same pan. Cook and stir until softened, 2-3 minutes.
  8. Stir in vinegar and jam. Simmer until syrupy, 1 minute.
  9. Stir in stock concentrate and 1/4 cup water. Cook until thick and glossy, 2-3 minutes.
  10. Turn off heat and stir in 1 tbsp butter. Season with salt and pepper.
  11. Toss mixed greens with a squeeze of lemon juice and a drizzle of olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
  12. Divide chicken between plates with roasted potatoes. Drizzle chicken with sauce and serve the salad on the side.
